This collaboration has followed from my recent post-doctoral Research Associate role at Imperial College London, based at the Royal Brompton Hospital (UK). I studied Physics (MSci) at King's College London (UK) and Biomedical Imaging (DPhil) at the University of Oxford (UK).

I'm also interested in the interplay between medical imaging and artistic design.
